# Detailed Guide: EIN & Business Banking for Timberline Collective LLC

## Part 1: Getting Your EIN (Employer Identification Number)

### What is an EIN?
An EIN is like a Social Security Number for your business. It's a 9-digit number (format: XX-XXXXXXX) issued by the IRS to identify your business entity for tax purposes.

### Why You Need It
- **Banking**: Required to open a business bank account
- **Taxes**: File business tax returns
- **Hiring**: Required if you hire employees or contractors
- **Business Accounts**: Needed for Stripe, PayPal Business, merchant accounts
- **LLC Protection**: Helps maintain separation between personal and business finances

### How to Get Your EIN

#### Option 1: Online (RECOMMENDED - Fastest)
1. **Go to**: https://www.irs.gov/businesses/small-businesses-self-employed/apply-for-an-ein-online
2. **Requirements**:
   - Valid SSN or ITIN
   - LLC name: "Timberline Collective LLC"
   - LLC address: 60175 NW RAILROAD AVENUE, TIMBER, OR, 97144
   - Responsible party: Your name (as registered agent)
   - Business type: Limited Liability Company
   - Reason for applying: Started new business
3. **Process**:
   - Fill out online form (takes 5-15 minutes)
   - Answer questions about your LLC
   - Submit application
   - **Receive EIN immediately** (displayed on screen)
   - Print/save confirmation page
4. **Hours**: Available Monday-Friday, 7am-10pm ET
5. **Cost**: FREE

#### Option 2: Phone
- **Number**: 1-800-829-4933
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 7am-10pm ET
- **Process**: Answer questions, receive EIN over phone
- **Time**: 10-20 minutes
- **Cost**: FREE

#### Option 3: Fax/Paper
- **Form**: SS-4 (available on IRS website)
- **Fax**: 855-641-6935
- **Time**: 4-5 business days
- **Cost**: FREE

### Information You'll Need
- **Legal Name**: Timberline Collective LLC
- **Trade Name/DBA**: Fur and Fame (if operating under this name)
- **Mailing Address**: 60175 NW RAILROAD AVENUE, TIMBER, OR, 97144
- **Business Type**: Limited Liability Company
- **Date Business Started**: 12-08-2025 (your registry date)
- **Responsible Party**: Your name (Colton Ray Cates)

- **Principal Activity**: Professional services / Digital services / E-commerce (choose closest match)

### After You Get Your EIN
1. **Save it securely** - You'll need it for everything
2. **Update your business records**
3. **Use it for bank account application**
4. **Keep confirmation letter** - IRS will mail you a confirmation letter in 4-5 weeks

## Part 2: Opening a Business Bank Account

### Why You Need a Business Bank Account
- **Legal Requirement**: Maintains LLC liability protection
- **Tax Compliance**: Easier to track business income/expenses
- **Professional**: Looks more legitimate to customers
- **Payment Processing**: Required for Stripe, PayPal Business
- **Separation**: Keeps personal and business finances separate

### What You'll Need

#### Required Documents:
1. **EIN Confirmation** (from Part 1)
2. **Articles of Organization** (your LLC registration document from Oregon)
   - You can download this from Oregon Secretary of State website
3. **Operating Agreement** (recommended, some banks require it)
   - Simple document outlining LLC structure
   - Can create a basic one yourself or use a template
4. **Personal ID**: Driver's license or passport
5. **Proof of Address**: Utility bill or bank statement
6. **Initial Deposit**: Usually $25-$100 minimum

#### Optional but Helpful:
- Business license (if you have one)
- Business plan (for some banks)

### Bank Options

#### Traditional Banks
**Pros**: 
- In-person service
- Established reputation
- Full banking services

**Cons**: 
- Higher fees
- More paperwork

### Application Process

1. **Choose Your Bank** (recommend Mercury or Novo for online business)
2. **Gather Documents** (EIN, Articles of Organization, ID, Operating Agreement)
3. **Apply Online or In-Person**
   - Online: Usually 10-20 minutes
   - In-person: 30-60 minutes
4. **Make Initial Deposit** ($25-$100 typically)
5. **Wait for Approval** (1-3 business days for online, same day for in-person)
6. **Receive Account Details** (account number, routing number)
7. **Order Checks** (if needed, optional)
8. **Set Up Online Banking**

### What to Do After Opening Account

1. **Connect to Stripe**: Update your Stripe account with business bank account
2. **Set Up Accounting**: Link account to QuickBooks/Wave
3. **Order Business Debit Card**: Use for business expenses only
4. **Set Up Alerts**: Monitor account activity
5. **Keep Records**: Save all statements and receipts

## Part 3: Operating "Fur and Fame" Under Timberline Collective LLC

### Do You Need a Separate EIN for Fur and Fame?

**NO - You do NOT need a separate EIN.**

Here's why:
- **Timberline Collective LLC** is your legal business entity
- **Fur and Fame** is a trade name (DBA - "Doing Business As")
- One LLC = One EIN
- You can operate multiple businesses/trade names under one LLC with one EIN

### How to Set Up "Fur and Fame" as a DBA

#### Option 1: Register DBA with Oregon (RECOMMENDED)
1. **File Assumed Business Name** with Oregon Secretary of State
2. **Cost**: ~$50
3. **Where**: https://sos.oregon.gov/business/Pages/default.aspx
4. **Why**: 
   - Legally allows you to operate under "Fur and Fame" name
   - Protects the name in Oregon
   - Required if you want to use "Fur and Fame" on bank accounts, contracts, etc.

#### Option 2: Just Use It (Simpler, but less protection)
- You can operate as "Fur and Fame" without formal registration
- Use it for marketing, website, social media
- Bank account will be in LLC name, but you can use "Fur and Fame" for customer-facing materials

### Recommended Approach

1. **Get EIN** for Timberline Collective LLC (one EIN)
2. **Open Business Bank Account** in LLC name: "Timberline Collective LLC"
3. **Register DBA** "Fur and Fame" with Oregon (optional but recommended)
4. **Use Both Names**:
   - Legal/formal: Timberline Collective LLC
   - Branding/marketing: Fur and Fame
   - Bank account: Timberline Collective LLC
   - Website/branding: Fur and Fame
   - Contracts: Can use "Timberline Collective LLC d/b/a Fur and Fame"

### Tax Structure

- **One EIN** = One tax return
- All income from Fur and Fame goes under Timberline Collective LLC
- File one Schedule C (if single-member) or Form 1065 (if multi-member)
- No separate tax filing needed

### Bank Account Setup

- **Account Name**: Timberline Collective LLC
- **Can Also Use**: "Timberline Collective LLC d/b/a Fur and Fame" (if DBA registered)
- **Checks/Statements**: Will show LLC name
- **Customer Payments**: Can show "Fur and Fame" on invoices/receipts

### Summary

✅ **One EIN** for Timberline Collective LLC  
✅ **One Business Bank Account** in LLC name  
✅ **Fur and Fame** operates as DBA/trade name under the LLC  
✅ **No separate EIN needed** for Fur and Fame  
✅ **Optional**: Register "Fur and Fame" as DBA with Oregon ($50)

## Questions?

Common questions answered:
- **Q: Can I use my personal bank account?**  
  A: Not recommended - breaks LLC protection, harder for taxes

- **Q: Do I need a separate account for each business?**  
  A: No - one account for the LLC, can track different businesses with accounting software

- **Q: When do I need to register the DBA?**  
  A: If you want to use "Fur and Fame" on legal documents, contracts, or bank accounts. Otherwise, optional for marketing use.

- **Q: Can I change the LLC name later?**  
  A: Yes, but requires filing amendment and may need new EIN. Better to keep LLC name and use DBA for branding.
