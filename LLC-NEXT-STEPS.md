## üî¥ Immediate Priority (This Week)

### 1. Get EIN (Employer Identification Number)
- **What**: Federal tax ID number for your LLC
- **Why**: Required for banking, taxes, hiring employees, opening business accounts
- **How**: Free from IRS
  - **Online**: https://www.irs.gov/businesses/small-businesses-self-employed/apply-for-an-ein-online (fastest - immediate)
  - **Phone**: 1-800-829-4933 (Monday-Friday, 7am-10pm ET)
  - **Fax/Paper**: Form SS-4
- **When**: Do this FIRST - banks require it
- **Cost**: FREE
- **Time**: 5-15 minutes online

### 2. Open Business Bank Account
- **What**: Separate bank account for business finances
- **Why**: Legally required to maintain LLC protection, keeps finances separate
- **What you need**:
  - EIN (from step 1)
  - LLC registration documents (Articles of Organization)
  - Operating Agreement (recommended, can create simple one)
  - Personal ID (driver's license)
- **Options**: 
  - Local banks (Chase, Bank of America, Wells Fargo)
  - Credit unions
  - Online banks (Mercury, Novo, Bluevine)
- **Cost**: Varies (some free, some have monthly fees)
- **Time**: 1-2 hours (including application and initial deposit)
