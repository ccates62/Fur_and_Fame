## Questions to Ask Yourself

1. **Do I need a business license?** Check your city/county requirements
2. **Do I need insurance?** General liability is recommended
3. **Will I hire employees?** Need workers' comp, payroll setup
4. **Will I sell physical products?** May need sales tax permit (Oregon doesn't have sales tax, but check)
5. **Do I need a separate business phone?** For professionalism
6. **Should I trademark "Fur and Fame"?** Protects your brand name
