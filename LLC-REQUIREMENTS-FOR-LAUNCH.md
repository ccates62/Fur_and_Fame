### 2. Register DBA (Doing Business As) for "Fur & Fame" ‚ö†Ô∏è **REQUIRED**

**Why you need it:**
- Your LLC is "Timberline Collective LLC"
- But you're selling as "Fur & Fame"

**How to register:**
1. **Go to:** https://sos.oregon.gov/business/Pages/assumed-business-name.aspx
2. **File Assumed Business Name** (DBA)
3. **You'll need:**
   - LLC name: "Timberline Collective LLC"
   - Assumed name: "Fur & Fame"
   - Your EIN (from step 1)
   - Business address
4. **File online** or download form

**Cost:** ~$50-100 (varies by county)  
**Time:** 30 minutes  
**Result:** Legal to use "Fur & Fame" name
