### 3. Oregon State Tax Registration üìã **REQUIRED**

**Why:** You need to register for state taxes.

**What to register for:**

#### A. Business Income Tax
- **Register at:** https://www.oregon.gov/dor

- **Cost:** Free to register

**Steps:**

2. Register your business
3. Get your tax ID number
4. Set up filing schedule

**Priority:** High (required before significant sales)
